Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how ReClaim It handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number
  • Login credentials and OTP authentication data
  • Lost or found item details and uploaded images
  • Location and report-related information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing lost and found reports
  • Verifying user identity
  • Supporting item recovery
  • Improving platform functionality

3. Data Sharing

Data is accessible only to authorized administrators or officials for recovery purposes. We do not sell or misuse user data.

4. Payment & OTP

Payment and OTP features are demo-based in this academic version. No real financial data is stored.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security practices. However, no digital system guarantees absolute protection.

This platform is developed for academic demonstration purposes.
